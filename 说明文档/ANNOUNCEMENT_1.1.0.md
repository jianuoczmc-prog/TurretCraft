# TurretCraft v1.1.0 — Announcement

## English

### Announcement

- TurretCraft **v1.1.0** is now available for Minecraft 1.20.1 (Forge).
- This update focuses on **Upgrades**, a new **Firmware/Manual** pair of items, improved **GUI stats & tooltips**, added **zh_tw**, and better **multiplayer projectile spawning**.
